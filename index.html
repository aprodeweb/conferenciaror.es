Conferencia Rails Madrid, 13-15th October 2016

What is this?

Conferencia Rails is the most venerable large scale Spanish Ruby and Rails related event. It has held 6 editions, spawned 1 spin-off sister js event, received +1.500 attendees and brought +100 speakers.

This year we will have some workshops on October 13th, and two days full of amazing talks on October 14th and 15th. The conference will take place in Madrid.

How to get there?

Friday's Cocktail Dinner

Friday's cocktail dinner and drinks will take place at The Irish Rover, a nice walk (or a couple subway stations) away from the talks venue. We will have a cocktail dinner and an hour of free drinks. The address is: Avenida de Brasil, 7 28020, Madrid and the nearest subway (Metro) station is Cuzco of Line 10 (dark blue).

Saturday's Drinks and live music

On saturday we have a live band at Moby Dick Club, as well as discount drinks to keep on networking well into the night, so be there and bring your friends! It is right next to friday's dinner pub: Avenida de Brasil, 5 28020, Madrid and the nearest subway (Metro) station is Cuzco of Line 10 (dark blue).

Who are the organizers?

Conferencia Rails is organized by the “Asociación para la Promoción del Desarrollo web” (APRODEWEB) a non-profit, volunteer-driven association to promote web development in Spain. It has been organizing a conference on Ruby and Rails for the past 6 years.
